Compliance & Certifications

Our Compliance Framework

At CyberShield Pro, we understand that trust is earned through transparency and adherence to the highest security standards. Our comprehensive compliance framework ensures that our security practices meet and exceed industry requirements, providing our clients with confidence in our ability to protect their most sensitive data and systems.

All our certifications are maintained through regular independent audits and continuous improvement processes, ensuring that our security posture evolves to address emerging threats and regulatory changes.

Our Certifications

ISO/IEC 27001:2022

Information Security Management System

What It Means:

ISO 27001 is the international standard for information security management systems (ISMS). It provides a systematic approach to managing sensitive company information, ensuring it remains secure.

Our Implementation:

  • Our ISMS has been independently audited and certified by an accredited third-party auditor
  • Regular risk assessments and treatment plans
  • Continual improvement of security controls and processes

Certification ID: CSP-ISO27001-2023-0452
Valid until: October 2025

GDPR Compliance

General Data Protection Regulation

What It Means:

GDPR is the EU's data protection and privacy law that governs how organizations process personal data of EU citizens, regardless of where the organization is located.

Our Implementation:

  • Data Protection by Design and by Default in all systems
  • Process for handling Data Subject Access Requests (DSARs)
  • 72-hour breach notification process
  • Data Processing Agreements with all partners

HIPAA Compliance

Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act

What It Means:

HIPAA sets the standard for protecting sensitive patient data in the healthcare industry, ensuring the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of electronic protected health information (ePHI).

Our Implementation:

  • Business Associate Agreements (BAAs) with healthcare clients
  • Access controls and audit trails for ePHI
  • Encryption of ePHI at rest and in transit

SOC 2 Type II

Service Organization Controls

What It Means:

SOC 2 is an auditing procedure that ensures service providers securely manage data to protect the interests of their clients and the privacy of their customers.

Our Implementation:

  • Regular independent audits of our security controls
  • Comprehensive security policies and procedures
  • Transparent reporting on control effectiveness

Audit Period: January 2023 - December 2023
Auditor: Deloitte & Touche LLP
