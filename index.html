SMS & Call Bombing Protection

Protect yourself from harassment through SMS and call bombing attacks. Learn how to identify, prevent, and stop these attacks.

What is SMS/Call Bombing?

SMS/Call bombing is a form of harassment where an attacker sends a massive number of SMS messages or calls to a target's phone number, causing disruption and stress.

How to Protect Yourself

  • Use call and SMS blocking apps
  • Enable Do Not Disturb (DND) mode during an attack
  • Contact your telecom provider to block the numbers
  • Avoid sharing your number publicly

Recommended Protection Apps

Truecaller

Hiya

Call Control

Powerful features for detecting and blocking call bombers

Should I Answer?

Offers excellent protection with offline databases

Immediate Actions During an Attack

  • Enable "Do Not Disturb" mode and allow only contacts
  • Use a call blocking app to filter unknown numbers
  • Contact your mobile carrier for assistance
  • Report the incident to authorities if it involves threats

Our Certifications

ISO/IEC 27001:2022

Information Security Management System

What It Means:

ISO 27001 is the international standard for information security management systems (ISMS). It provides a systematic approach to managing sensitive company information, ensuring it remains secure.

Our Implementation:

  • Our ISMS has been independently audited and certified by an accredited third-party auditor
  • Regular risk assessments and treatment plans
  • Continual improvement of security controls and processes

GDPR Compliance

General Data Protection Regulation

What It Means:

GDPR is the EU's data protection and privacy law that governs how organizations process personal data of EU citizens, regardless of where the organization is located.

Our Implementation:
