Understanding Cyber Threats

Phishing Attacks

Deceptive attempts to obtain sensitive information by disguising as a trustworthy entity in electronic communication.

  • Spear phishing targets specific individuals
  • Often uses urgent or threatening language
  • May contain malicious links or attachments

Malware

Malicious software designed to disrupt, damage, or gain unauthorized access to computer systems.

  • Viruses, worms, and trojans
  • Spyware and keyloggers
  • Often spread through email attachments

Ransomware

Malicious software that blocks access to a computer system until a sum of money is paid.

  • Encrypts files and demands ransom
  • Often targets businesses and organizations
  • Can cause significant downtime and data loss

DDoS Attacks

Distributed Denial of Service attacks overwhelm a system with traffic, making it unavailable to users.

  • Uses multiple compromised systems
  • Targets websites, servers, and networks
  • Can be mitigated with proper infrastructure
