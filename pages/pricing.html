Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our pricing and services.

Can I change plans later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. When upgrading, you'll be charged the prorated difference. When downgrading, changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

Is there a free trial?

We offer a 14-day free trial for our Professional plan. No credit card required. Experience our full suite of security features before committing.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers for annual contracts.

Do you offer discounts for non-profits?

Yes, we offer a 25% discount for registered non-profit organizations. Contact our sales team with your non-profit registration details.

What's your cancellation policy?

You can cancel anytime. No long-term contracts. If you cancel mid-cycle, you'll have access until the end of your billing period.
