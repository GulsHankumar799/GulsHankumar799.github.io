1. Introduction

CyberShield Pro ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website cybershieldpro.com and use our services.

🔒 Our Commitment: By accessing or using our Service, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to be bound by all the terms of this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you:

  • Register for an account
  • Request information about our services
  • Subscribe to our newsletter
  • Contact our support team
  • Participate in surveys or promotions

This information may include:

  • Name and contact information (email address, phone number, address)
  • Company information (name, size, industry)
  • Payment information (processed through secure third-party providers)
  • Communication preferences

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, we automatically collect certain information about your device:

  • IP address and geolocation data
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Referring URLs and pages viewed
  • Time spent on pages and clickstream data

2.3 Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience. You can control cookies through your browser settings, but disabling them may limit some functionality.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Service Delivery: To provide and maintain our cybersecurity services
  • Transaction Processing: To process payments and manage subscriptions
  • Communication: To send security alerts, updates, and support messages
  • Improvement: To enhance our website and services based on usage patterns
  • Security: To detect, prevent, and address technical issues and threats
  • Compliance: To fulfill legal obligations and regulatory requirements

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

4.1 Service Providers

We share information with trusted third parties who perform services on our behalf:

  • Payment processing (Stripe, PayPal)
  • Data analysis and analytics
  • Email delivery services
  • Cloud hosting and infrastructure
  • Customer support platforms

4.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law or valid requests by public authorities.

4.3 Business Transfers

In case of merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your information may be transferred with notice provided.

5. Data Security

We implement enterprise-grade security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: AES-256 encryption for data at rest and TLS 1.3 for data in transit
  • Access Control: Multi-factor authentication and role-based access
  • Monitoring: 24/7 security monitoring and threat detection
  • Audits: Regular security assessments and penetration testing
  • Incident Response: Documented procedures for security incidents
