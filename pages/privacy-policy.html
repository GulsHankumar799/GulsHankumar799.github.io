1. Introduction

At CyberShield Pro, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our website and services. By using CyberShield Pro, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Our commitment: We're committed to being transparent about how we handle your data. If you have any questions, you can always contact us at privacy@cybershieldpro.com.

2. Information we collect

2.1 Information you give us

You may provide us with information when you:

  • Create an account or use our services
  • Request customer support or contact us
  • Subscribe to our newsletters or marketing
  • Participate in surveys, research, or promotions
  • Register for events or webinars

This information may include your name, email address, phone number, company name, payment information, and any other details you choose to provide.

2.2 Information we get from your use

When you use our services, we automatically collect:

  • Device information: IP address, browser type, operating system, device identifiers
  • Usage data: Pages visited, features used, time spent, clicks, and navigation patterns
  • Log data: Error reports, performance data, and system activity
  • Location information: Approximate location based on IP address

2.3 Cookies and similar technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to improve your experience, analyze traffic, and personalize content. You can manage cookies through your browser settings. Learn more in our Cookies Policy.

3. How we use information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Provide and maintain our services: To deliver our cybersecurity solutions and process transactions
  • Improve and develop: To understand usage patterns and enhance our features
  • Communicate with you: To send security alerts, updates, and support messages
  • Security and fraud prevention: To detect and prevent threats, abuse, and illegal activity
  • Legal compliance: To meet our legal and regulatory obligations
  • Personalization: To customize your experience and show relevant content

4. Information sharing

4.1 With your consent

We share your information when you ask us to or give us permission.

4.2 With service providers

We work with trusted third parties who process data on our behalf:

  • Payment processors (Stripe, PayPal)
  • Cloud infrastructure (Google Cloud, AWS)
  • Analytics providers
  • Customer support tools
  • Email communication services

4.3 For legal reasons

We may share information if required by law, legal process, or to protect rights and safety.

4.4 Business transfers

If CyberShield Pro is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale, your information may be transferred with notice.

5. Data security

We implement strong security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: Data encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256)
  • Access controls: Strict authentication and authorization protocols
  • Monitoring: 24/7 security monitoring and threat detection
  • Audits: Regular security assessments and penetration testing
  • Compliance: SOC 2 Type II, ISO 27001 certified

Note: While we strive for perfect security, no method of transmission is 100% secure. We continuously update our practices to stay ahead of threats.

6. Data retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. We may retain data longer to comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, or enforce agreements. When we no longer need data, we securely delete or anonymize it.
